📌 Project Overview
This is an end-to-end data analysis project that explores 19,000+ job postings scraped from Naukri.com for Data Science and Data Analytics roles across India.
The goal was to uncover real, actionable insights about the Indian job market — what skills are in demand, which cities are hiring, what experience companies prefer, and what the fresher job landscape looks like.
This project covers the complete data analyst pipeline:
Data Collection → Cleaning → EDA → SQL Analysis → Power BI Dashboard

🎯 Key Business Questions Answered

🏙️ Which cities have the most Data Science & Analytics job openings?
🛠️ What technical skills do companies demand the most?
📅 What experience level is most in demand?
🏢 Which companies are hiring the most?
🆚 How do Data Analytics and Data Science roles differ?
🎓 What does the fresher job market actually look like?
💰 How transparent are companies about salary?
🌐 How many jobs are remote vs onsite?

💡 Key Insights
🏙️ Location Insights

Bangalore dominates with 4,165 job openings — clear #1 tech hub
Hyderabad, Mumbai and Gurgaon follow as strong secondary markets
Remote jobs account for only 5.3% of total postings

🆚 Role Insights

Data Analytics has 51% of all job postings — far more than Data Science
Data Science accounts for only 11.8% of postings
DA roles are significantly more accessible for freshers

📅 Experience Insights

Mid Level (3-5 Yrs) has the highest demand with 8,200+ jobs
Junior (1-2 Yrs) comes second with 6,000+ jobs
Fresher roles account for 1,426 jobs — competitive but possible!

🛠️ Skills Insights

SQL and Python are the most consistently demanded skills
Machine Learning appears in 2,681 job postings
Power BI and Excel remain highly relevant for DA roles
Data Analytics and Analytical skills mentioned across majority of postings

🏢 Company Insights

Coders Brain Pvt Ltd is the top hirer with 318 postings
Accenture and Diverse Lynx follow closely
Accenture leads fresher hiring with 36 fresher openings

🎓 Fresher Insights

934 total fresher job openings across India
Bangalore is the top city for freshers with 349 jobs
Data Analytics accounts for 75.7% of fresher jobs vs DS at 24.3%
92.7% of fresher jobs do NOT disclose salary — transparency is very low
